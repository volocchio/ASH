Why Sponsor ASH?

Your brand becomes part of one of the most emotionally powerful, visually stunning, and widely shared events a family will ever experience.

🎥 Documemorial Reach

Every ceremony is broadcast live across 7 platforms — YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, X, LinkedIn, and direct stream. Your sponsor recognition reaches every viewer, everywhere, forever.

❤️ Emotional Resonance

Ash dispersal ceremonies are among the most moving moments in a family's life. Sponsors associated with ASH are remembered as partners in something sacred — not just a logo on a banner.

✈️ Unique Content Asset

Aerial footage of skydiving formations, balloon releases at sunset, and soaring dispersals over iconic landscapes generates millions of organic views. Your brand is embedded in that content.

501(c)(3) advantage: All corporate sponsorships are fully tax-deductible. ASH provides IRS acknowledgment letters for all contributions over $250.
