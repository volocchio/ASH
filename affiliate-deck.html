How the Partnership Works

Simple, low-friction referral model. You serve your client. We handle the ceremony. Everyone wins.

1 — You Refer

Your team introduces ASH at the right moment — during hospice planning, at cremation selection, in estate planning, or at point of life insurance claim. We provide you with a simple referral toolkit.

2 — We Deliver

ASH handles the entire ceremony from consultation to Documemorial production. Your client receives a world-class, deeply personal aviation farewell — you take zero operational risk.

3 — You Earn

Affiliates receive a referral fee on every completed ceremony. High-volume partners qualify for tiered revenue sharing and co-branded marketing materials.

No operational complexity: You don't manage aviation, dispersal logistics, or Documemorial production. You simply connect families to a service they will be forever grateful for — and receive compensation for every completed ceremony.
