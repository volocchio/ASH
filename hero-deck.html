The Sacred Mission

You've spent your life mastering the sky. Now use that mastery to give families a farewell they will never forget.

"Every life deserves a farewell that soars. The ASH Guild makes that possible — with skill, reverence, and the courage to fly into the most emotional moment of a family's life."

🎯 What You Do

You conduct aviation ash dispersal ceremonies — skydiving formations, helicopter flyovers, balloon releases, BASE descents, and more — using ASH's proprietary dispersal vessel, trained to our protocols.

🦉 Who You Join

The ASH Guild is a brotherhood and sisterhood of aviators who believe their skills serve a higher purpose. You are not a vendor. You are a Guardian of Final Flights — trained, certified, and honored.

💛 Why It Matters

37% of cremated remains sit on a shelf — families unable to find a meaningful way forward. Every ceremony you conduct frees a soul to soar and gives a family peace that nothing else can provide.

Your Discipline Has a Place

Every aviation discipline is welcome in the ASH Guild. Your specific skills determine which ceremonies you lead.

What Guild Membership Gives You

This is not just a gig. The ASH Guild offers meaningful compensation, community, purpose, and recognition for elite aviators.

💰 Compensation

  • Per-ceremony fees ($200–$2,000+ depending on type)
  • Bonus for complex formations and international expeditions
  • Revenue sharing for Guild members who recruit others
  • Travel & accommodation covered for destination ceremonies

🎓 Training & Certification

  • 2-day ASH Dispersal Device intensive
  • Ceremonial protocol and family interaction training
  • Weather decision-making and safety procedures
  • Annual recertification with updated protocols

🦉 Recognition & Community

  • Official ASH Guild certification and digital badge
  • Featured in Documemorial credits
  • Annual ASH Guild gathering & awards
  • Private Guild network and communications channel
  • "Guardian of Final Flights" — a title you earn

Purpose beyond the jump: Guild members consistently report that ASH ceremonies become the most meaningful flights of their career. The families never forget you. Neither will you forget them.

The Right Aviator

Technical skill is required. But the best ASH Guild members bring something more — a heart for service and the ability to hold space for a family in their most vulnerable moment.

You Must Have

  • Current certification in your discipline (see requirements)
  • Clean safety record — no significant incidents
  • Current medical certificate (where applicable)
  • Valid insurance coverage
  • Genuine compassion for families in grief
  • Professionalism in appearance and communication

You Will Bring

  • Pride in your craft and the ASH mission
  • The ability to adapt to each family's needs
  • Respect for the sacred nature of each ceremony
  • Commitment to ongoing training and recertification
  • A network of other qualified aviators to recruit
  • Your unique story — every Guild member is a hero

We are not a gig economy platform. ASH Guild members are trained, certified, recognized professionals. We invest in you because you represent the highest expression of what ASH stands for — the skill, courage, and heart to give a final farewell the sky it deserves.
